Tony Stark

The one and only Iron Man

Proof that Tony has a heart

  • 1970 - Born in Manhattan, New York.

  • 1983 - At age 14 he attended Massachusetts Institute of Technology: MIT for two years.

  • 1991 - His parents went away to the Bahamas, both are killed in a car accident which was later revealed that they were assassinated by Winter Soldier. As a result he took over his father's company as the CEO of Stark Industry.

  • 2010 - Stark is wounded and imprisoned by a terrorist group he was able to escape with a powerful armor and with the sacrifice of Ho Yinsen. After his escape, he announced that Stark Corp will no longer manufacture weapons and he created an improvised armor suit as well as a more powerful arc reactor.

  • 2011 - He discovers that the palladium core in the arc reactor that keeps him alive and powers the armor is slowly poisoning him. Then with a hidden message from his father, he discovers a new element, which could be used as the core of his arc reactor.

  • 2012 - He joined the Avengers to stop Asgardian Loki that seizes the Tesseract from a S.H.I.E.L.D. facility and the alien's Earth invasion wich later to be known is the Thanos's army.

  • Also 2012 - Stark orders J.A.R.V.I.S. to remotely destroy all of the Iron Man suits as a sign of his devotion to Potts, and undergoes surgery to remove the shrapnel embedded near his heart.

  • 2015 - Stark and Bruce Banner discover an artificial intelligence within the scepter's gem, and secretly decide to use it to complete Stark's "Ultron" global defense program. Which was a huge mistake as they unintentionally created an AI thet tried to kill them and destry the world. Which the Avengers then stopped.

  • 1945 - Discovers a way to grown wheat twice each season, doubling wheat yields

  • 2016 - After revealing that Barnes(Winter Soldier) killed Stark's parents, Captain America and Iron Man had and intense fight ended with Stark's armor disabled and Captain America and Winter soldier leaving.

  • 2018 - Tried to stop Thanos from collecting all the six infinity stones but failed to do so. Which results in half of the universe population turns into dust.

  • 2023 - After return to Earth, Stark chooses to retire and raise his daughter. He decided to go back to Avengers headquaters when he found out the way to redo all this mess. And in the last battle decided to sacrifice himself to win the battle against Thanos
